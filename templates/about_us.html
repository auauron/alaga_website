About Alaga

Empowering families and caregivers to navigate life-limiting illnesses — with the best care.

Our Mission

Alaga is designed to help family members and caregivers manage care profiles for loved ones with chronic conditions or elderly individuals who need assistance.

Our mission is to simplify the caregiving process, reduce stress, and improve health outcomes through organized care management.

OUR JOURNEY

Our Story

How Alaga came alive.

Alaga was born from personal experience. I, Sophia Mendoza, the founder, witnessed firsthand the challenges of caring for multiple family members with chronic conditions - my Lolo suffering from stroke from my Mom's side, my Lola from rheumatic diseases, and my other Lolo from Parkinson's from my Dad's side. Growing up in a household of caregivers, doctors, and nurses, I have felt the overwhelming stress of managing medications, appointments, and health data across multiple people. These people may be gone now, but they inspired me to create this platform for others.

We created Alaga to address these challenges and provide a comprehensive solution for caregivers and families, yet is still budget-friendly and easy to use. The name "Alaga" comes from the Filipino word meaning "to care for" or "to nurture," reflecting our commitment to supporting those who provide care to others.

"To love is to care. Even when it's hard. Even when it hurts."
— Sophia Mendoza, Founder
